Sprites

At Kenney.nl you can download game assets free of charge, you do not have to credit but it is a nice gesture.

Vector art

vecteezy.com is a website for vector art, graphics and illustrations some free and some not. The ones we use are made by Shawn

Music and sound

This game uses these sounds from Freesound.org key pick up sound by Alexander and rock on pressure plate by MikeKoenig

Freemusicarchive.org is a webpage for free music, our background music (Visager - Forest Fanfare) and our victory music (Komiku - Finaly Together) are both taken from this website. Sadly it has recently closed down from lack of funding.

Fonts

Fontmeme.com is a fonts & typography resource where you can browse, filter, custom preview and download free fonts.

Web development

W3schools.com The worlds larges website for developers

Stackoverflow.com a online community for developers to learn, share their knowledge, and build their careers. Google any problem and Stackoverflow will have the answer.

Miscellaneous

Mailchimp.com is a marketing website that helps grow your business, we have implemented a E-mail subsciption form from them.

Wufoo.com a website for making forms, our questionaire and feedback is hosted by them.
