Storyline!



King Sverre is put to the test when he wants to marry his
long lost love Jürgen of Preussens, but to win Jürgens
hand, Sverre will have to go through his biggest test
yet. The unbeatable Preussens-Labyrinth. The test
seems harder than expected, but Sverre soon realizes
that if Jürgen is what he truly wants, he has to risk
his life. Is love strong enough?
